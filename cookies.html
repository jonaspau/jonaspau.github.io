Cookes are the greatest

What can I say. They taste great and they store information for later.

I have currently implemented Piwik PRO for testing on this site. It consists of a tag manager and some analytics scripts.

What cookies are used?

Piwik uses three main cookies:

    _pk_id_appID.domain
    _pk_ses_appID.domain
    _ppms_privacy_appID

The id and ses cookies both have a lifetime of 30 minutes as I do not store a user ID. These are used to compile analtyics reports based on aanonymous users

The ppms cookie has a lifetime of 12 monnths and is used to store your consent data to avoid asking you the next time.
